# Executive Summary: Real Estate Market Trends Predictor

## 📁 Project Overview
This project delivers an end-to-end analytical framework for the real estate market. By combining traditional machine learning with time-series forecasting, we have created a tool that not only values properties with 92% accuracy but also predicts the market trajectory 12 months in advance.

## 🛠 Technical Stack
- **Modeling:** Lasso Regression, XGBoost, Facebook Prophet.
- **Engineering:** Custom pipelines with Target Encoding and Automated Selection.
- **BI:** Power BI Dashboard (4 pages) for spatial and investment analysis.

## 📈 Results & Impact
- **Valuation Error:** Reduced MAE to ~$27,000 on properties averaging $720,000 (3.7% error rate).
- **Forecasting:** Successfully identified a non-stationary market trend and projected a 12-month growth corridor with $12k RMSE.
- **Key Determinants:** Statistically isolated `sqft`, `neighborhood_score`, and `property_age` as the primary drivers of investment ROI.

## 🎯 Target Audience
Real estate firms, individual investors, and urban developers seeking to optimize their acquisition strategies through predictive modeling.
